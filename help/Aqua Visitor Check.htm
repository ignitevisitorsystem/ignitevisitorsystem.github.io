Aqua Visitor System

The Aqua Visitor System is comprised of a web-based scheduling app, iPad app, and name badge printer that allows Aqua employees to schedule and visitors to check-in and check-out with a touchless experience.

Update Visitor Schedule Active/In-Active

back to top

By default, all schedules are active. However, there may be a schedule that is on hold or cancelled all together. In those cases, you need to make schedule In-Active.

1.    Go to aquavisitorsystem.github.io & click on View Scheduled Visits (see #1 in image below)

2.    Enter in your Aqua Network Login ID [example: ckonkol] (see #1 in image below)

3.    Click blue button (see #2 in image below)

4.    Select schedule to make in-active/active by clicking the Click here link. (see #1 in image below)

5.    Click the link below Status to change status to active or in-active (see #1 in image below)

 

Check-In/Check-Out Using iPad App

back to top

 

Use the below steps to check-in or check-out!

 

1.    Tap or Scan

a.    Have a QR Code? Great! Scan QR Code by showing to the iPad camera! (see image below)

b.    Do not have a QR Code? Great! Tap the green button! (see image below)

                                          i.    Select your schedule by tapping Select link (see image below)

                                         ii.    To Check-in OR Check-out: Tap Here to Check-in/Out (see image below)

2.    Check-In: Upon successful check-in, retrieve name badge from printer! (see image below)

3.    Check-out: Upon successful check-out, dispose of name badge before leaving reception/lobby! (see image below)

Reports

back to top

 

1)    Todays visitor schedule: aquavisitorsystem.github.io/?all=today

2)    Schedules

a.    Active

                                          i.    All Active schedules: aquavisitorsystem.github.io/?all=yes

                                         ii.    User Active schedules: aquavisitorsystem.github.io/?Active=Yes&userid=[Login ID]

Example: aquavisitorsystem.github.io/?Active=Yes&userid=ckonkol

b.    In-Active

                                          i.    All In-Active schedules: aquavisitorsystem.github.io/?all=no

                                         ii.    User In-Active schedules: aquavisitorsystem.github.io/?Active=No&userid=[Login ID]

Example: aquavisitorsystem.github.io/?Active=No&userid=ckonkol
